My New Life Starts Now! Hulk Hogan, 70, and Sky Daily, 45, Prove That Third Times the Charm

Posted by Tandra Barner on Wednesday, August 28, 2024

One of the most well-known wrestlers in the world, Hulk Hogan, has bet on love for the third time in his life, and seems like he hit the jackpot. The 70-year-old sports legend is a living proof that age means nothing when it comes to love as his new wife is 25 years younger than him. Let’s learn more about Hogan’s tumultuous love history, and the woman that healed his heart.

His first marriage gave him a beautiful family.

In 1983, Hulk Hogan married Linda Claridge, which gave the start to one of the most well-known unions in wrestling history. The couple had a very long relationship together, seemed like they were each other’s endgame. Hulk and Linda had two children: daughter Brooke and son Nick, born in 1988 and 1990, respectively.

Owing to Hogan’s wide popularity, the family started their own reality show called Hogan Knows Best. Unfortunately, in 2007, reports of Hulk’s infidelity came to light, which turned out to be true. Linda decided to end the marriage after 24 years.

His second marriage restarted his life.

Hulk’s divorce was initiated in 2007 and finalized in 2009. Reportedly, he was having an extremely hard time after the separation, but fate intervened. Not long after Linda filed for divorce, Hogan found a new reason to live and managed to get engaged once again just a few months after he was officially a single man.

In early 2008, the wrestler began dating Jennifer McDaniel, who then became his wife in a little under two years. Jennifer was a makeup artist, but shared Hogan’s love for fitness.

Unlike Hulk’s ex, Linda, Jennifer was 21 years his junior, but that didn’t matter to the couple. Hulk and Jennifer were officially married in 2010. Their union lasted until 2022, when the wrestler announced their divorce on Twitter.

His third marriage might be his forever.

At the same time as Hogan announced that him and Jennifer McDaniel were divorced, he revealed the identity of his new girlfriend. As he shared on Twitter: “Just for the record, the Facebook and Instagram posts are of me and my girlfriend Sky, I am officially divorced, sorry I thought everyone already knew.” And just like that, in early 2022, Hulk was already dating his next wife.

In July 2023, it became known that Hulk and Sky were engaged. The happy couple didn’t waste any time and tied the knot two months after announcing their engagement. At 70 years old, Hulk is on another love journey and can’t be any happier. He posted the wedding pics on his Instagram with the caption: “My new life starts now!”

Who is Hulk’s new wife?

Sky Daily is the woman who stole mighty Hulk Hogan’s heart for the third time in his life, but what do we know about her? Sky is 25 years younger than her beloved wrestler, but, as we know from experience, age doesn’t matter to Hulk. She works as a yoga instructor, so fitness is a big thing they have in common.

Daily is also a mom to three children, adding to Hogan’s two adult ones. Reportedly, the famous wrestler met Sky’s kids before the wedding and got along with them quite well. They can be seen in attendance of the couple’s wedding.
