10 Celebrity Couples Who Tied the Knot in 2022

Posted by Kelle Repass on Monday, August 5, 2024

2022 was a year full of highlights in the world of entertainment. We saw Beyoncé surprising everyone by releasing a new album and the second installment in the Avatar saga finally premiering 13 years after the first one. But for a few celebrities, the year also gave them something to celebrate in their personal life: getting married! We now look back at some of the famous couples who got their happily ever after in 2022.

Britney Spears and Sam Asghari

After meeting for the first time in 2016 on the set of one of Britney’s music videos and hitting it off, it took the couple nearly 5 months to reconnect. But once they did, their relationship kept growing until Asghari proposed to Spears in September 2021. Finally, the 2 said “I do” in a star-studded ceremony in June 2022.

Taylor Lautner and Taylor Dome

The star of the Twilight movies, Taylor Lautner, got married in November 2022, about 1 year after getting engaged. The lucky woman is Taylor Dome, a registered nurse whom Lautner met in 2017 at a game night at his home. And in an amusing coincidence, after already sharing the same first name, the couple now also shares their last, making them both Taylor Lautner.

Kirsten Dunst and Jesse Plemons

Hahn Lionel/ABACA/Abaca/East News, VALERIE MACON/AFP/East News

The pair of actors met on the set of their show Fargo in 2015 while playing a married couple. And 6 years after they started dating, they tied the knot in real-life in a private ceremony in 2022.

Kourtney Kardashian and Travis Barker

The oldest Kardashian sister, Kourtney Kardashian, and Blink-182’s drummer, Travis Barker, had not 1, not 2, but 3 weddings in 2022. The first time the pair exchanged vows was in an intimate ceremony in Las Vegas, in April. Then, in May, they had a second wedding in Santa Barbara, California. Finally, the couple surrounded themselves with loved ones for a third ceremony that took place overseas, in Portofino, Italy.

Ewan McGregor and Mary Elizabeth Winstead

Invision/Invision/East News

Similar to what happened with Kirsten Dunst and Jesse Plemons, McGregor and Winstead met on the set of Fargo. Back then, in 2016, both were still married to other people. They each ended their previous relationships and got together. In June 2021, they welcomed their first child together, and in April 2022, the couple privately tied the knot.

Rita Ora and Taika Waititi

The singer and renowned director went public with their relationship in 2021, appearing together at the premiere of The Suicide Squad in August of the same year. About one year later, the couple quietly got married.

Anya-Taylor Joy and Malcolm McRae

Known for her role in The Queen’s Gambit, Anya-Taylor Joy is now a married woman. She and Malcolm McRae, a musician, became husband and wife in an intimate courthouse wedding in July 2022.

Alexandra Daddario and Andrew Form

Actress Alexandra Daddario from the hit TV show The White Lotus married producer Andrew Form in New Orleans, in June 2022. The 2 met after passing each other on the street in New York City, and Form took the initiative to ask her to dinner. The engagement then came in August 2021.

Bonnie Wright and Andrew Lococo

The actress who portrayed Ginny Weasley in the Harry Potter movies, Bonnie Wright, announced on Instagram in March 2022 that she had gotten married. The news came with a video that showcased her and her husband’s wedding rings and a caption that read, “Yesterday was the best day of my life.”

Ben Affleck and Jennifer Lopez

Invision/Invision/East News, OnTheJLo via Grosby Group/Grosby Group/East News

After a whirlwind romance in 2002, when Affleck and Lopez got engaged, that ended 2 years later, the famous duo rekindled in 2021. In an interview, the actress and singer claimed their breakup was the biggest heartbreak of her life. She also let the world know that now, 20 years later, their love story has its happy ending after the 2 tied the knot in August 2022. “True love does exist, and some things do last forever,” she said.
