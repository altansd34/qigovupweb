How old is Bruce Boxleitner?

The actor was born on 12th May 1950 in Elgin, Illinois, USA. Bruce Boxleitner age is 70 years old as of 2020. He is the son of Cliff Boxleitner, a certified public accountant and Diane Boxleitner.

Education

After his elementary education, he joined Prospect High School in Mount Prospect, Illinois. He later enrolled in the Goodman School of Drama at the Art Institute of Chicago, currently known as The Theatre School, at DePaul University.

Career

The actor began his career in 1973. He played the role of Rick Welsh, a University of Minnesota track star, in one of the episodes of The Mary Tyler Moore Show. In 1975, he starred as Bobby Joe in the film Sixpack Annie, and this marked his debut in the film sector.

His debut on TV shows started when he played the role of Luke Macahan in How the West Was Won between 1976 and 1979. He then played Billie Joe Robbins in the 1980 film The Baltimore Bullet.
