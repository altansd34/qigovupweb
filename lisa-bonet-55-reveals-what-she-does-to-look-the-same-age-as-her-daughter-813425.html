Lisa Bonet, 55, Reveals What She Does to Look the Same Age as Her Daughter

Posted by Kelle Repass on Sunday, August 11, 2024

At 55 years old, Lisa Bonet continues to radiate youthfulness and beauty. With flawless skin and a vibrant glow, she effortlessly defies the passing years. Lisa Bonet’s secret to looking young lies in her dedicated skincare routine, healthy lifestyle choices, and a genuine zest for life. Her ageless beauty serves as an inspiration to many, proving that age is just a number when it comes to maintaining a youthful and captivating presence.

She uses coconut oil as a body lotion.

Gilbert Flores/Broadimage/Broad Image/East News

Something as easily affordable as coconut oil can replace many creams and lotions and do the job just as well. It can keep your skin hydrated and relieve signs of inflammation.

The Road to Paloma actress says she likes coconut oil because it’s free of harsh chemicals. “I use organic coconut oil, which is what I use on my body as well,” she shared. “It’s simple and pure. Why put a lotion on your body that has 10 ingredients when you can put on one that is from nature and smells divine and does the job?”

She keeps her brows natural.

Admedia, Inc/Sipa USA/East News, Evan Agostini/Invision/East News

Transforming the shape of your brows has the incredible power to sculpt your face, giving you a timeless allure that can effortlessly take years off or infuse a youthful radiance. Bonet says she likes to keep her brows intact.

“My brows are natural. I guess I got lucky. If you overtweeze, they might not grow back. I’ve always kept them quite full. It’s amazing how brows can change your face,” she said.

She takes good care of her sensitive skin.

Bonet admits her skin is quite sensitive, and it’s important to choose the skincare products wisely to avoid irritation.

“I use pretty active things at night to keep the cells turning over. I use a scrub a couple of times a week. Then I’ll use a couple of serums. I also have really sensitive skin that requires a good amount of care. I’m not one of those people who can go to sleep with their makeup on and wake up and be fine,” she shared.

She stays active.

Invision/Invision/East News

Regular exercise has numerous benefits for your well-being and can even help prevent premature skin aging. The actress shares that, despite her excellent physical condition, she primarily leans towards low-impact exercises.

“My favorite form of fitness is dance. I take an African dance class that I live for. I just walked in one day, and I have been doing it pretty regularly for seven years now. I also do Pilates twice a week, yoga once a week, and I go to the gym twice a week,” she said.

Despite Lisa Bonet and Jason Momoa going their separate ways, their love story is truly heartfelt and unforgettable. Jason Momoa’s admiration for Bonet blossomed at the age of eight when he first saw her on a TV series. Today, they embrace the role of co-parents, cherishing their unbreakable connection while raising their two children.
