Emily in Paris Outfits From All Seasons, Ranked From Least to Most Creative

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, August 18, 2024

It’s fair to say the outfits of the bubbly protagonist, played by Lily Collins, vary from stunning and sophisticated to outlandish and bold throughout each season of the hit Netflix show, Emily in Paris.

Ahead, we ranked our favorite colorful, daring, and flamboyant looks the fashionista is seen in throughout the 3 seasons as she waltzes around Paris. Scroll on to see them all.

22.

In this scene, Emily is sporting a tweed skirt and a ruffled red crop top. It’s a surprisingly simple look for the over-the-top Emily.

21.

The daring fashionista donned a semi-vintage look with graphic prints and neon brights, topping it off with a colorful jacket.

20.

Emily channeled the mod fashion movement with a colorful print and flared silhouette. This statement coat feels like it’s straight out of the ’60s.

19.

Emily donned a black and white geometric bodycon outfit in this scene. The bustier-inspired bust and accompanying shoulder pads give the piece a space-age edge.

18.

Our bubbly Em wore a mini corset dress this time, completing the look with an eye-catching flouncy statement purple jacket. What better attire for a French garden party than a dress that looks like a blooming flower?

17.

Emily perfectly pulled off a plaid-on-plaid-on-plaid look, wearing a plaid jacket, a plaid top, a plaid skirt, and a newsboy cap.

16.

The stunning Emily donned a midi dress featuring huge puff sleeves and a very Gen-Z wavy design.

15.

Emily is no stranger to stepping out in bold and vibrant colors. And here, she is opting for a head-to-toe fuchsia look.

14.

The fashionista walked around like some sort of human tourism billboard in this Eiffel Tower-printed blouse and Paris-emblazoned pair of high heels.

13.

A preppy Emily sported a lemon-colored peacoat and a blush-toned lace blouse, adding a flower print pair of high socks to the look.

12.

Miss Cooper rocked this houndstooth beret moment while donning a printed mini dress, completing the look with a sporty pink and black jacket.

11.

Here, Emily is opting, again, for really bold colors, wearing a chic sweater paired with a bicolor metallic skirt.

10.

The fashionista took a ‘60s approach to her style with these whimsical itsy bitsy printed shorts and a polka-dotted blouse, topping it off with a metallic coat.

9.

The bold protagonist is mixing different styles, once again, wearing a white lace blouse, topping it with a sport-printed top, and completing the look with a wildly cute red and white hat.

8.

This colorful floral look with a bright pink beret is surely the perfect embodiment of Emily’s playful and hopeful side.

7.

Here, Em is rocking a huge pink bow-adorned mini dress, pairing the strapless piece with a kimono from home.

6.

Even with more traditional attire, like this simple blazer, Emily is still opting to boost its impact by adding to it clashing patterns and neon colors.

5.

The elegant protagonist looks like she’s wearing “poetry” in this black off-the-shoulder dress and chic shiny clutch. The whole look seems to pay homage to Audrey Hepburn.

4.

Here, Emily is wearing “too many shoes!” It’s kitschy but cute. We’re very into this playful coat and bejeweled collar.

3.

Emily wore a cut-out dress from Magda Bultrym with very fun Casadei platform heels. Of course, the true star of this look is the stunning feathery coat.

2.

Even though this one’s not an Emily outfit, per se, she still looks incredible in this sculptured white couture gown.

1.

The maximalist queen strikes again! Emily turned heads at a high-fashion couture event wearing an extravagant statement zebra-print top with structured shoulders that almost look like wings. It’s an absolute work of art.

Have you already watched the much anticipated season 3 of the show? If you haven’t yet, who do you think Emily will end up with at the end of the season, the gorgeous Gabriel or the equally handsome Alfie?
