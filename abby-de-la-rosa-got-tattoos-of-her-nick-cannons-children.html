Abby De La Rosa Got Tattoos of Her, Nick Cannon's Children

Posted by Tandra Barner on Saturday, August 17, 2024

TALENTRECAP.COM Entertainment Celebrity

Photo by Phillip Faraone/Getty Images for REVOLT, CR: Michael Becker / FOX

Reading Time: 2 minutes

Abby De La Rosa recently shared a heartwarming tribute to her three children. The special moment took place at the Golden State Tattoo Expo, where De La Rosa, accompanied by her close friends and the ever-present Nick Cannon, decided to get their kids’ names tattooed on her skin.

Abby De La Rosa Tattooed Her Children’s Names

In these pictures, posted to De La Rosa’s account, fans see Cannon closely observing one of the children’s names etched on his baby mama’s wrist. The love and connection between them are evident in these moments.

De La Rosa proudly displayed her commitment to her children, with daughter Beautiful’s name on her wrist and son Zillion’s name inked on her mid-forearm. While it seems that they also added Zion’s name to her wrist, the photos don’t show it off clearly.

As we celebrate De La Rosa’s beautiful gesture of love, we can’t help but wonder if Cannon, the well-known host of Wild N’ Out, also decided to get his children’s names permanently inked. Recently, Cannon added “Legendary” to his tattoo collection, in honor of his growing family.

In a playful TikTok video from earlier this month, The Masked Singer host had a mock call from “Baby Mama #25,” with his phone ringing incessantly with reminders to pay child support. With humor and charm, Cannon silenced the call and continued with his paperwork, proving that even with a large family, he maintains a sense of humor and grace.

Nick Cannon Also Got a Tattoo of His Child With Bre Tiesi

While it’s unclear if Cannon also got tattoos of his and De La Rosa’s children, he recently made another significant tribute to his expanding family. Cannon and Bre Tiesi both celebrated their roles as parents by getting matching tattoos dedicated to their 14-month-old son, Legendary Love Cannon.

This thoughtful gesture had been in the works since Tiesi’s pregnancy, waiting for the right moment and artist to bring it to life. The tattoos, featuring their son’s name elegantly scripted in black ink, held deep meaning for the couple. Tiesi chose to have her tattoo placed on the side of her neck, while Cannon opted for his chest as the canvas for this permanent declaration of love.

Although their decision to etch their son’s name into their skin drew admiration from fans and friends, including Selling Sunset co-star Chrishell Stause, it was a quiet and personal celebration of parenthood. Tiesi shared glimpses of their decision-making process for the tattoos on Snapchat, emphasizing the significance of these marks on their journey of parenthood.
