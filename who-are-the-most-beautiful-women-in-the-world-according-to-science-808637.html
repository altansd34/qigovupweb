Who Are The Most Beautiful Women in the World According to Science

Posted by Patria Henriques on Wednesday, August 14, 2024

Even though we agree that ’’beauty is in the eye of the beholder,’’ there are scientific measures by which we can calculate the amount of facial attractiveness. The formula is known as the golden ratio of beauty, which dates back to Ancient Greece. Fast forward to our current times, where this equation is still heavily used by facial cosmetic surgeons. One of them, Dr. Julian De Silva, has decided to compile a list of the 10 most beautiful women in the world, sharing exactly how close they came to the ancient Greeks’ idea of perfection.

Bright Side is fascinated by the idea of measuring facial features with concrete numbers. We will share the celebs who science determined as having the best facial proportions. It can also be exciting to check if your own personal beauty preferences match this scientific formula.

10. Cara Delevingne — 89.99%

Is that supposed to be no makeup vs makeup? Because the former is awful, the latter is terrific.

The famous 30-year-old model is mostly known for her thick, full eyebrows that allowed her to make it to the top 10 list along with her nicely shaped lips, which scored a whopping 98.5%. However, Dr. Silva explained that she would’ve made it even higher on the list if she had a smaller forehead.

9. Katy Perry — 90.08%

She’s ok i guess but i know loads of ppl that are prettier than her besides she had tons of plastic surgery

According to the golden ratio proportions, the 37-year-old performer’s nose is her best trait — especially the base of her nose in proportion to her lip width, which scored 98.8%. Her chin had the lowest percentage compared to her other features. Her face shape is also considered one of her strong points.

8. Natalie Portman — 90.51%

Better looking with the hair down without all the makeup !

With a score of 99%, the 41-year-old actress’s eyes are positioned really well. Her brow area and lips, however, both scored the lowest among her striking facial features.

7. Scarlett Johansson — 90.91%

Invision/Invision/East News, Invision/Invision/East News

The 37-year-old actress got a nearly perfect score of 99.9% for her eye position. Her chin is her second-best feature, scoring 99.4%. She scored the lowest for her face shape and thin lips.

6. Kate Moss — 91.05%

Invision/Invision/East News, Invision/Invision/East News

The 48-year-old supermodel’s forehead and eye spacing are 2 of her biggest facial advantages. However, according to the golden ratio proportions, her brow area and face shape scored the lowest.

5. Taylor Swift — 91.64%

The first picture on the left is the best !

The 32-year-old singer’s chin and nose scored the highest, according to the golden ratio proportions, with both features scoring 97%. With 83%, her eyebrows scored the lowest among her other traits.

4. Ariana Grande — 91.81%

no she should be 10th

The 29-year-old pop star’s forehead, nose, chin, and lips are among her strongest facial features, according to the golden ratio proportions. Her eyebrows scored the lowest.

3. Amber Heard — 91.85%

With a 99.6% score, the 36-year-old actress has an almost perfect chin. Her nose also scored very high. At 80.2%, her face shape got the lowest score compared to her other features.

2. Beyoncé — 92.44%

The 40-year-old star has a nearly perfect face shape with a score of 99.6%, according to the golden ratio proportions. Her eyebrows and nose scored the lowest compared to her other facial features.

1. Bella Hadid — 94.35%

The 25-year-old model scored the highest for her chin, with 99.7%, followed by her eye position with 97.65% and her forehead with 97%. Her lowest scores came for her brow area and eyebrows.

Do you agree with this list? Which gorgeous woman would you add to it?
