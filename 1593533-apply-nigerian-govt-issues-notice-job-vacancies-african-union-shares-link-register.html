APPLY: Nigerian Govt Issues Notice of Job Vacancies in African Union, Shares Link to Register

Posted by Kelle Repass on Wednesday, August 14, 2024
  • Citizens of African Union (AU) member states who want to contribute to the development of the continent can apply to join the prestigious organisation
  • First, interested persons need to register and create their new job profile to apply for the vacant positions
  • Legit.ng reports that presently, the AU is offering more than 20 jobs with openings in Abuja, Addis Ababa, and other cities

Legit.ng journalist Ridwan Adeola Yusuf has over 9 years of experience covering public journalism.

FCT, Abuja - The Ministry of Foreign Affairs on Tuesday, May 21, urged qualified citizens to apply for job vacancies in the African Union (AU).

In a notice signed by Eche Abu-Obe, the spokesperson of the ministry, authorities notified Nigerians that the AU is seeking to recruit qualified candidates for 24 vacant positions "with duty stations in Addis Ababa - Ethiopia, Abuja - Nigeria, Libreville - Gabon, Nairobi - Kenya, and Lusaka - Zambia.
