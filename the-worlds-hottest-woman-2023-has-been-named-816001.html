The Worlds Hottest Woman 2023 Has Been Named

Posted by Tandra Barner on Saturday, August 24, 2024

Maxim Australia has finally issued its annual Hot 100 list for 2023. It names the top most prominent and beautiful women in the entertainment, sports, and pop culture industries. For many fans, knowing the winner’s name was an immense joy, and we were amazed to read the names not only behind the 1st place.

The number of beautiful women in one edition is overwhelming.

Maxim Australia collected famous women from so many spheres and made a top-100 list, and people are amazed by the variety of beautiful ladies represented in the list.

The comedian Celeste Barber, famous for her parodies of the most notable looks of the celebs, took 47th place.

Former Bachelor star Abbie Chatfield, 28, took the ninth place, while Married At First Sight star Evelyn Ellis landed 10th place.

In the meantime, Kayne West’s wife, Bianca Censori, took the 18th place on the list.

The couple have been making headlines with their long vacation in Italy, which was full of scandalous outfits worn by Bianca.

The Voice Australia host Sonia Kruger took place 36, while Nicole Kidman took the 48th position.

The 2nd place went to the world of sporty people.

MAXIM Editor-in-Chief Santi Pintado said, “This year, the definitive list of Australia’s most influential, successful, powerful, newsworthy, talented, and beautiful women features an eclectic mix of high-profile names such as Margot Robbie, The Matildas, Delta Goodrem, Kylie Minogue, Sophie Monk, Penny Wong and many more.”

He added, “From athletes to actresses, comediennes to TV personalities, musicians to politicians, and news presenters to models and influencers, the 2023 Ultra Tune MAXIM HOT 100 list covers a fantastically diverse field that won’t disappoint our loyal followers and all Australians alike.”

The second place went to Samantha Kerr, a woman who may not be so popular in the beauty industry, catwalks, and movies but is making a history in Australian football. Sam is a professional player, and throughout her career, she’s been rocking her professionalism and is a real star.

The 1st place was expected for many people.

On the top of the list is actress Margot Robbie, 33. Her Hollywood career shines brightly following the release of her latest film, Barbie.

Barbie became Warner Bros’ highest profit-making film, made $537 million at the US box office, and brought $1.2 billion in cinemas worldwide.

There’s an interesting fact about the actress, who appeared to be on the top list in 2023. It marks the second year in a row and the record fourth time that she has topped the Hot 100.
