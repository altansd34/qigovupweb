Kendall Jenner, Bad Bunny Reportedly Break Up After Less Than a Year of Dating

Posted by Patria Henriques on Thursday, August 22, 2024

TALENTRECAP.COM Entertainment Celebrity

All Photos by: Taylor Hill/Getty Images

Reading Time: 3 minutes

It seems like Kendall Jenner and Bad Bunny will be welcoming 2024 apart. The supermodel and rapper have reportedly broken up after 10 months of being together.

Kendall Jenner, Bad Bunny Split After 10 Months Together

An insider told People that Jenner and Bad Bunny have decided to call it quits ahead of the new year. At the time of the initial report, no particular reason for their breakup was cited. Now though, other insiders are speaking up, telling what the real reason of the split might be.

Recently, an unnamed source told ET that Jenner and Bad Bunny have been busy doing their own businesses and “things slowly started to fizzle out between them.”

“They both knew going into this that it likely wouldn’t be a forever type of relationship and that was mutually understood from the get-go,” the source said. “They have crazy busy schedules and know they’re still young and have a lot more to experience individually before settling down.”

In addition, the source said that there “isn’t any negativity between them and they still want the best for one another.”

The supermodel and Puerto Rican superstar first sparked romance rumors in February. Fans began speculating about their relationship status after they were seen joining Hailey Bieber and Justin Bieber on a double. During that time, an insider reported that they were introduced after Bad Bunny moved to Los Angeles.

Since late October, the pair hadn’t been seen bonding together. This was when Jenner supported the rapper at his Saturday Night Live hosting debut in New York City. On December 16, The supermodel was spotted having fun with friends in Aspen, Colorado, without Bad Bunny.  

Reports Say Her Family Doesn’t Have Anything Against the Rapper

Despite the pair’s alleged breakup, Jenner’s family is reportedly cool about the split. The source stated that the supermodel’s relatives don’t have any bad blood against the musician.

“Kendall’s family still thinks highly of him,” the source said. “They just want her to be with whoever makes her happy, treats her with love and respect, and someone that understands and can handle the attention that comes along with dating her with poise and grace.”

Although their relationship was inevitably made known to the public, Jenner and Bad Bunny made efforts to keep their relationship private. Previously, however, the socialite shared her belief when it comes to love.

“I love really hard, and I love without apology,” she told Harper’s Bazaar in an interview published August. “I don’t like goodbyes, and I will fight to not have to say goodbye, I will always fight for relationships. I’ve been that way since I was little, although I was shy and sometimes very closed off,”

“I don’t give up on anything. Some people aren’t willing to meet me at that level,” she added. “But that’s okay. I’d rather do that than shut myself off to something and not give it a proper chance.”
