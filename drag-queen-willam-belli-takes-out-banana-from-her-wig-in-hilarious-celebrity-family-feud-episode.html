Drag Queen Willam Belli Takes Out Banana From Her Wig in Hilarious 'Celebrity Family Feud' Episode

Posted by Tandra Barner on Wednesday, August 21, 2024

TALENTRECAP.COM Entertainment Celebrity

ABC/Matt Miller

Reading Time: 2 minutes

Drag queens are never running out of playful gimmick, even on a different competition show. In a recent Celebrity Family Feud episode, drag diva Willam Belli took a fresh banana out of her wig, shocking everyone with such effortlessly funny move.

Willam Belli Opens Her Wig, Took a Banana Out on Celebrity Family Feud

On Sunday, cast members of Drag Me To Dinner faced off against a team of drag queens for the coveted prize of $25,000. The competition began relatively wholesome, but things went totally crazy when Belli was called to stand in front of the buzzer.

After she and David Burtka performed a customized hand shake, Belli, opened her hair and pulled out a banana. The audience then burst into laughter, contrary to host Steve Harvey who showed a stunned reaction. Belli took a bite then gave the banana to Burtka, who responded by also consuming a chunk of the fruit. When Belli handed the banana to Harvey, the host remained bewildered, prompting the drag queen to just toss the banana aside.

“You thought it was a banana in my wig, but I was just happy to see you,” Belli said.

After the funny moment, fans went to Twitter to express their reaction. On fan tweeted that the episode had “gone bananas” while others pointed out Harvey’s humorous reaction to Belli’s gimmick.

Things Got NSFW During the Game

After the bizarre banana scene, things got even more interesting. The next category of the game for guessing went completely NSFW, hilariously eliciting entertaining answers from the contestants.

“Something that married people do that a dog might do while making love,” Steve said.

Belli quickly pressed her buzzer, saying “Eat a–,” with the last word getting censored for broadcast.

The next category to be guessed in the game was “Blank daddy.” Drag queen Manila Luzon, who immediately pushed her buzzer, then answered “Sugar daddy!”

The game continued to be playful in the next scenes, with drag queen Symone responding “Big daddy” to Steve. Such was coupled with the host’s silly reply.

“I would day I’ve been called that before, but I don’t think this is the time for that,” he said.

The Team Drag Queens eventually took home the $25,000, purposely donating the same to ACLU’s Drag Defense Fund.
