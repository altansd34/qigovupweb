Chayce Beckham is a new man on his latest single “Tell Me Twice.” The singer is embracing his new life after dealing with his past struggles. “Tell Me Twice” premiered today and fans could not be more excited to hear new music from the Season 19 American Idol champion.

The four minute song serves as a life lesson for us all. “Tell Me Twice” has a theme that teaches us to take the time we need to slow down and also be grateful. The singer opens up about the troubles he faced while working in his hometown. Chayce speaks on the life lessons he has learned from taking advice. On “Tell Me Twice,” the singer now knows what is important.

The American Idol alum is reflective on “Tell Me Twice.” The advice Chayce mentions that he took could be from his parents, who first told him to try out for Idol. The singer was open throughout Season 19 about his past addiction struggles and credits his parents for helping him through. Now Chayce is ready to give advice to others on the things in life we should love as well as appreciate.

“Tell Me Twice” shows Chayce in a new light. The often somber singer is upbeat on his latest single. Chayce says that his mother was the one who encouraged him to write “Tell Me Twice,” once the singer moved to Nashville. Chayce also announced that his latest single is the first of many to be released soon.

Country Music’s Brightest Star, Chayce Beckham

Chayce is proving he is not just another Idol winner to come and go. The country crooner’s fanbase has continued to grow. Chayce plays to sold out shows nightly and shared the stage with American Idol judge Luke Bryan last summer. As Idol Season 19 was gearing up for its finale, Chayce mentioned that he had a whole album ready to go.

Now that he is in Nashville, fans can rejoice to get the singer’s first studio album. If history repeats itself, Chayce’s first album will come just in time before Idol Season 20 ends.

The next leg of the singer’s tour kicks-off this month. Fans can next see Chayce as a mentor for Season 20 Idol contestants during the upcoming Hollywood Week episode.
