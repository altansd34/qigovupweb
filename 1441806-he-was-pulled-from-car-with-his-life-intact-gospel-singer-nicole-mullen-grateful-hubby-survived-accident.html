He Was Pulled From the Car With His Life Intact: Gospel Singer Nicole Mullen Grateful Hubby Survived

Posted by Billy Koelling on Friday, August 23, 2024
  • The revered musician and songwriter took to social media to thank God for sparing her husband's life
  • She shared photos of a badly damaged car her husband was travelling in when he was involved in the road carnage
  • Her fans camped on the comments section of her post to sympathise with her even as they prayed for her hubby

American singer, songwriter, and choreographer Nicole Mullen has come out to thank God for saving her husband's life.

Thank you Lord

The My Redeemer crooner took to social media on Wednesday morning, November 3, to share about how her hubby escaped death by a whisker following a grisly road accident.

In a Facebook post seen by Legit.ng, the musician shared photos of the wreckage that is the car her husband was in when the unfortunate incident happened.

In the caption, Mullen pleaded with her fans and followers to pray for her husband who is currently recuperating following the accident.
