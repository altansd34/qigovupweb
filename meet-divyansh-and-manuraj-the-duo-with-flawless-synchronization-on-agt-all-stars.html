Meet Divyanshand Manuraj, The Duo With Flawless Synchronizationon 'AGT All Stars'

Posted by Patria Henriques on Wednesday, August 14, 2024

Photo by: Trae Patton/NBC

Reading Time: 2 minutes

Ever since Divyansh and Manuraj first appeared on TV, viewers have been left amazed by the memorable performances of the beatboxer and flautist duo. As the crowned the winners of India’s Got Talent season nine, this amazing duo will bring a surprising performance at America’s Got Talent All Stars.

They Won India’s Got Talent Season 9

Divyansh and Manuraj are best known as India’s Got Talent season nine winners, and they are excellent champions. They have performed as a duo both domestically and internationally, and are now well known across the world. They come from Rajasthan in India, and they certainly know how to put on a show that the masses enjoy.

The musical duo put in a lot of effort to compete on India’s Got Talent in 2022. The pair said in an interview with India Today that they were confident in their abilities. It was truly their hard work and determination that helped them win.

The secret behind their outstanding routine is truly their tremendous love of music. It helps them create and perform their amazing routines on stage. In addition, Divyansh and Manuraj have admitted that their unique styles made it difficult to create their act. They indicated that they had to make multiple tweaks over the season, but luckily, it was all worth it in the end.

Divyansh & Manuraj on Winning India’s Got Talent

During IGT season nine, Manuraj performed impressively on the flute, and Divyansh captivated the audience with his beatboxing abilities. They were able to deliver memorable performances even after receiving the Golden Buzzer at the Auditions.

“Ever since we came on this show, our aim was not to win. But to reach out to the masses, present something new and make more people interested in the music we create,” The duo said.

The judges and audience were wowed by the pair’s chemistry and stage presence. They were ultimately named the winners of the Indian reality TV program in 2022 and received 20 lakhs in winnings. Carrying their confidence on their backs, and having the support of millions, Divyansh and Manuraj are going into AGT All Stars fully intending to win the show.
