Meet 'Romanii Au Talent's Darci Lynne Farmer, 13 Year Old Ana Maria Margean

Posted by Kelle Repass on Tuesday, August 27, 2024

Romanii Au Talent

Reading Time: 2 minutes

Ana Maria Margean was the 11-year-old ventriloquist who stole Europe’s heart when she took the stage of Romanii Au Talent. This year, she intends to repeat this success but this time, on AGT All-Stars. Let’s get to know her more.

Ana Maria Margean Won Romania’s Got Talent Season 11

Since she stepped into the Roman version of the Got Talent franchise, Ana Maria Margean was a force to be reckoned with. This young performer stood out not only due to her appealing vocals, but also because of her impressive ventriloquy with her puppet Lizzie. Amazingly, she received Andra’s first and only Golden Buzzer during her audition.

Speaking to their local newspaper, Margean described her Golden Buzzer moment as “the most beautiful day in my life.” With that, she said that she’s thankful for the judge.

“When I went on stage, I didn’t expect to go directly to the semi-final, but I’m extremely happy that Andra pressed the golden button,” she said (translated to English).

She further stunned the crowd when she brought two puppets, Lizzie and Nicole, during the Grand Finale. Together, the sang a remix of “One Night Only” by Jennifer Holiday, and “Listen” by Charice Pempengco. She was hailed as Season 11 winner after this striking performance. In addition, Margean took the grand prize worth 120,000 euros.

She Says Joining AGT All-Stars is “One of Her Biggest Dreams”

Margean discovered her passion for singing at a very young age of five. She was a part of the musical theater ensemble of her school and has become a part of Bucharest’s Operetta and Musical Theatre Ion Dacian.

Evidently, Margean wants her singing talent to be known all around the world. According to her, she dreams to become a “complete artist one day,” delighting people’s souls through music and other artistic acts.

She may be another step closer to this vision this year. Last November, Margean announced that she’s among the diverse contestants joining America’s Got Talent spin-off series, AGT All-Stars.

“One of my biggest dreams is coming true!” she wrote. “I will be on AGT #AGTAllStars stage for the first time in my life, in front of the amazing judges Simon Cowell, Howie Mandel, Heidi Klum, and the unique Terry Crews!”

If you want to witness Margean’s journey on AGT All-Stars, be sure to tune in on January 30, on NBC.
