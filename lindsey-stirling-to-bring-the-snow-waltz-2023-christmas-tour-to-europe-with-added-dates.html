Lindsey Stirling To Bring The Snow Waltz 2023 Christmas Tour To Europe With Added Dates

Posted by Kelle Repass on Tuesday, August 6, 2024

TALENTRECAP.COM Entertainment Celebrity

Photo by Jeff Hahne/Getty Images

Reading Time: 2 minutes

World renowned electronic violinist, dancer, and artist Lindsey Stirling has announced her highly anticipated Snow Waltz 2023 North American tour. In a recent Instagram video post, Stirling and her team expressed their excitement about extending the tour to Europe.

Lindsey Stirling Added European Dates To Her Christmas Tour

In a recent Instagram video, Stirling and her team shared that they will be extending their Snow Waltz 2023 tour to Europe this holiday season. The video captures the candid reactions of Stirling and her team upon learning about the new tour dates.

Their reactions, filled with surprise and enthusiasm, highlight the importance of this exciting announcement. From London and Paris to Brussels, The Netherlands, and Berlin, Germany, Stirling will bring her holiday magic to European audiences. Extending the holiday tour to Europe is a first for Stirling, and the anticipation is certainly felt within the posts.

The European leg of the Snow Waltz tour begins on Dec 18, 2023, at London’s Eventim Apollo. The tour will then continue with performances in Paris, France, Brussels, Belgium, Utrecht, the Netherlands and Berlin, Germany. These added European dates promise holiday enchantment on a global scale.

The full tour’s grand finale will then be held in Chicago, Illinois, on December 31, 2023, at the Auditorium Theatre. This New Year’s Eve show promises to be an unforgettable way to bid farewell to the old year and welcome the new one. It is bound to feature Stirling’s signature style and holiday charm.

Lindsey Stirling Has Been Doing Guest Performances Before Her Tour Starts

Before embarking on her highly anticipated Snow Waltz 2023 Christmas Tour, Stirling graced the stage in a memorable guest performance. Teaming up with Canadian indie pop sensation Walk Off the Earth, Stirling enchanted the audience at the Ford Wyoming Center on August 25.

Her signature choreographed violin performances combined with Walk Off the Earth’s creative energy set the stage on fire, offers fans a taste of what they can expect during her upcoming tour. These guest appearances are a just the beginning to what promises to be an extraordinary holiday season filled with enchantment.

Last year, Stirling released of her captivating Christmas album, Snow Waltz, under the Concord Records label. This album beautifully blends Stirling’s signature complexity in musical arrangements with an exuberant energy that also captures the true spirit of the festive season.
