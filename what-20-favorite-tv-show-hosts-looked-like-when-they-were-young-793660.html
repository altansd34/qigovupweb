What 20+ Favorite TV Show Hosts Looked Like When They Were Young

Posted by Billy Koelling on Wednesday, August 7, 2024

Now they’re witty, engaged, or even controversial, always dressed up and sometimes wearing makeup, but talk show hosts weren’t always like this. Before they were famous, America’s biggest TV show hosts looked completely different than they do now. Even celebrities like David Letterman, Jay Leno, and Oprah have pictures from their younger years that you might have never seen.

For your enjoyment, Bright Side compiled a series of photo collages below of famous talk show hosts’ now & then.

7. Craig Ferguson — The Late Late Show with Craig Ferguson

8. John Oliver — Last Week Tonight

9. James Corden — The Late Late Show with James Corden

10. Seth Meyers — Late Night with Seth Meyers

11. Phillip Schofield — This Morning

12. Chelsea Handler — Chelsea Lately

13. Larry King — Larry King Live

14. Kelly Ripa — Live with Kelly and Ryan

15. Ryan Seacrest — Live with Kelly and Ryan

16. Oprah Winfrey — The Oprah Winfrey Show

17. Wendy Williams — The Wendy Williams Show

18. Anderson Cooper — Anderson Cooper 360°

19. Katie Couric — 60 Minutes

20. Samantha Bee — Full Frontal with Samantha Bee

21. Ellen DeGeneres — The Ellen DeGeneres Show

22. Jay Leno — The Tonight Show
