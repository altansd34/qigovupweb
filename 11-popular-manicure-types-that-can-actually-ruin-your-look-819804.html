11 Popular Manicure Types That Can Actually Ruin Your Look

Posted by Kelle Repass on Sunday, August 18, 2024

When we communicate with people, they don’t look at our face only. They also pay attention to our hands. That is why it is so important to have well-groomed nails and a decent manicure. However, not everything that seems trendy and attractive is really so. We decided to find out what kinds of manicure are no longer in fashion or can even ruin your look.

French manicure

It seems that the classic French manicure with a transparent coating and a white nail tip will never go out of fashion. However, according to experts, this type of manicure looks old-fashioned in 2024. Nevertheless, it can be refreshed by introducing other shades or unusual color combinations instead of white.

Acrylic nails

Manicure, made in this technique, always implies nail extensions. Acrylic nails can be very long and allow you to apply on them various types of nail art, including 3-D compositions.

However, according to experts, this type of manicure has long been out of fashion. In addition, it is extremely uncomfortable to do chores with it. Gel coating can become a good substitute for acrylic nails. It also has a more natural and well-groomed look.

Any type of glitter

Manicure with glitter, both large and small, has long been hopelessly outdated. Some nail artists also claim that if each nail is covered with something shiny, it looks defiant. Therefore, experts advise avoiding this manicure this year.

Nail stickers

Not so long ago, stickers on nails were a notable trend. However, over time, many people have realized that stickers start peeling off very quickly and the manicure looks extremely unkempt.

Manicure experts suggest favoring nail art instead. You can also resort to an unconventional method: use dried flowers covered with clear varnish on top.

Duck nails

Duck nails suddenly became popular in 2021. However, experts don’t seem to be willing to call this manicure ultra-trendy today. Moreover, manicurists believe that nails in the shape of duck feet look unnatural, as well as unsafe for everyday life.

Magnetic nails

The iridescent effect is most often achieved with the help of magnetic nail polish. However, this product also creates an unwanted shine, as if there are small glitters on the nails. And lately, experts advise against glittery manicures.

Dark nail polishes

Dark nail polish can look beautiful and perfectly complement a look. But there is one catch: polishes of such colors as black, dark blue, burgundy can highlight any creases and pigment spots on the skin. In addition, they make the skin of the hands look pale and dull.

Therefore, experts believe that the trend of using dark nail polishes at a mature age is already outdated. The older a person is, the more carefully they should choose the shade. According to experts, it’s best to use nude and neutral tones (beige, light pink, gray) or, on the contrary, eye-catching colors, like bright red.

Manicure with piercing

When the trend of pierced nails first appeared, many people thought it was cool. However, very soon both beauty bloggers and ordinary people began to note that this manicure looks rather strange than beautiful. Besides, if you don’t know how to pierce nails correctly, you can ruin them.

Crackle polish

When a manicure cracks naturally, it’s considered sloppy. However, this hasn’t stopped the trend for manicures with artificially made cracks. It is also called crackle polish.

The idea at first seems interesting and extraordinary. In fact, this manicure is far from elegant and looks too sloppy.

Black and white manicure with marbled effect

The manicure itself with this effect looks really fresh and cute. But experts advise leaving black and white shades behind in favor of less boring colors, like soft pink, green and blue colors.

Floral patterns

Dried flowers on the nails maybe look elegant. But active prints in the form of flowers, on the contrary, have long been considered out of style. This nail art doesn’t bring variety, but resembles wallpaper.

Bonus: some colors defy fashion

If you are not interested in the latest trends, the right way is to make a monochrome manicure without embellishments. And it’s worth choosing the shade that is always on trend. These colors include red, light pink, brown, bright blue, beige, and gray.
