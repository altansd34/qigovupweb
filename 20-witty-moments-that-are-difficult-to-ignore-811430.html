20 Witty Moments That Are Difficult to Ignore

Posted by Kelle Repass on Thursday, August 29, 2024

Don’t forget to take a picture of a comical moment when you spot it. A while later, you will scroll through your photo gallery and just laugh about the fact that it happened to you. Even a woman driving in the next car can make your day and stay in your memory forever.

1. “The sign on the car says, ’Stop texting while driving.’”

2. “My friend woke up to this in Gros Morne National Park, Newfoundland, Canada.”

3. “I woke up to the sound of someone shouting, ’It’s Christmas, it’s Christmas!’ I look outside and see this.”

4. “Pigeon poops portrait of itself on a leaf.”

5. “I wonder which song was on.”

6. “I spotted this today at the Cleveland Museum of Natural History.”

7. “All the single ladies!”

8. “I saw these girls at IKEA. What were they thinking?”

9. “I don’t think my mom understands how her rice cooker works.”

10. “So close yet so far.”

11. “I thought I lost $350, and then 3 months later, I found it in my 4-year-old’s room.”

12. “I’ve heard of ’painting yourself into a corner’ but my wife took it a step further. I don’t even know.”

13. “It’s time to take a Spooky dookie.”

14. “My daughter told me we only had a little milk left in the fridge.”

15. Just a normal day to wear a box.

16. “Spotted during the second inning of a game today.”

“Dumb question — How long does a baseball game last?”

17. A solution has been found.

18. A stranger on the metro

19. “Mountain bikes only!”

20. Woodpecker be like, “You took everything from me!”
