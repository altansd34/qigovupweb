7 Shockingly Easy Nail Designs You Can Totally Do at Home

Posted by Kelle Repass on Wednesday, August 28, 2024

As women, most of us can agree that there is nothing better than the feeling of getting our nails done. Many of us enjoy going to the salon for a manicure, but sometimes the cost adds up quickly.

The good news is that with a little effort and a small investment, you can treat yourself from home. Nail art is hard, but these easy nail designs are fit for even the most inexperienced nail artists. Check out some of the best simple nail art ideas ahead.

1. Easy nail design inspired by ocean waves

This cool nail design will remind you of the ocean breeze. To achieve this look at home, you will need white, turquoise, and blue nail polish, a sponge, a plastic cup of water, nail polish remover, hairspray, and some cotton pads. Now let’s get started with this easy nail design!

  • On the sponge, you will need to apply the turquoise and blue nail polish, drawing a line, turquoise below and blue above. This will allow you to create a cool ombre nail design.
  • Once you have your nails ready, apply the sponge to each one of them. When you finish this step, let them dry, and in the meantime prepare the plastic cup with water for the next step.
  • Put a few drops of white nail polish in a cup of water, and then spray some hairspray on top.
  • Now dip each nail into the water and carefully pull them out so that your nails can have a cool beach waves-inspired nail design. With the cotton pad, remove the excess, and that’s it!

2. Add chrome powders to create a cute easy nail design.

Chrome powders are becoming popular for all nail art lovers who are looking for simple nail designs. Chrome powders are super easy to use, and you can create as many different manicure ideas as you want! To use them, all you need to do is to follow these easy steps:

  • Prepare and apply a base coat of your favorite color on your nails.
  • Then apply a top coat or, as in the example above, draw a few lines where you want the chrome powder to be.
  • Now that your nails are done, you can achieve this easy nail design by carefully dabbing the chrome powder where you want it on your nails with an applicator brush.
  • Let them dry, and use a dusting brush to remove any loose dust. Apply another layer of top coat and there you have it — a cute nail design!

3. Fun nail design inspired by Pop Art

This fun nail design can be easily achieved at home and will make your nails pop like a comic strip. First, you have to apply a base coat of your favorite color — you can let your imagination run wild here!

  • Apply 2 coats and let them dry. Then, using a fine brush, outline the nails with black polish.
  • And now, here’s the part where this cool nail design gets interesting. Using your creativity and inspiration from your favorite comics, draw lines and dots with a fine brush. There is no limit here! To finish, end with a layer of top coat, and voilà!

4. A cute and easy rainbow nail design

This rainbow manicure idea is 100% easier than it sounds. All you need to do is create an ombre effect on your nails using a sponge and then apply a top coat of glitter polish.

  • To get this easy nail look, choose your favorite nail polish colors and apply them to a small sponge by drawing lines on top of each one.
  • Apply the sponge to each nail. Repeat this step until you get the desired design.
  • Finish off with a top coat of glitter polish, and let your nails shine!

5. Minimalist simple nail art design

When you want to keep it simple yet stylish, this easy nail idea is for you. You will start with a nude base coat. Then do French tips on each nail.

  • To achieve this, you can use nail tape or a silicone nail stamper so that you can get the perfect curved French manicure.
  • Once you let your French tips dry, proceed with a fine brush to draw some vertical lines on the right side. What makes this cute and simple nail design interesting is the combination of black and white lines. But you can also add your own spark and creativity by mixing your 2 favorite colors.

6. Easy nail art design for the holidays

Another variation on the classic French, Christmas-inspired nail design. Because we never get tired of glittery nails, this easy nail design calls for your favorite glitter nail polish and some Christmas-inspired stickers.

  • Start with a base coat, and then apply the glitter nail polish to the top of your nails, similar to how you would a French manicure.
  • The difference here is that we’re not going for a perfect line, so don’t worry, you can even add a bit of glitter to the rest of the nail.
  • Then select your favorite stickers and stick them on each nail. Now, let the Christmas spirit shine on your nails!

7. Freehand fun nail design

Because there is nothing that is as much fun as creating your own nail designs, this easy nail art was made for you to show off your inner artist.

  • To achieve this freehand technique, we recommend first practicing swirl designs on paper with some detailed brushes. Once you feel confident enough, let’s move on to your nails.
  • Make swirls with the brush on your nails and add color details with a fine brush.
  • Using a dotting tool, make little dots, and just blend between the lines, swirls, and dots in your manicure!
