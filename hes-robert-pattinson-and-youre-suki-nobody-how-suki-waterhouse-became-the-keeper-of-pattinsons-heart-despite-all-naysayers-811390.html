Hes Robert Pattinson and Youre Suki Nobody, How Suki Waterhouse Became the Keeper of Pat

Posted by Patria Henriques on Thursday, August 22, 2024

There are couples in Hollywood that keep their relationship so openly public, we know almost every detail of their private lives. We follow them as if they were the contestants of a reality show.

Robert Pattinson and Suki Waterhouse, on the other hand, are not at all like most public lovebirds in the world of showbiz. Happiness loves silence, and they know this for sure. Here’s how they manage to stay together despite all the stereotypes and toxic opinions.

From the start, the couple has been very secretive about their relationship.

Robert Pattinson and Suki Waterhouse were first seen together as a couple in July 2018. Before their first big public appearance in 2022, they were very low-key about their relationship. Since 2018, the 2 lovebirds have been busy bonding and spending plenty of quality time together.

“They are very loved-up,” a source told People about the couple at that time. “They haven’t been together that long. But everything seems on the fast track. They are always laughing and smiling at each other.”

Once, Pattinson shared on Jimmy Kimmel Live! that he watched The Batman for the first time alongside Waterhouse, and that her sweet and cute reaction to the film “kind of changed the entire thing.”

“I’m pretty sure she’s not normally into watching superhero movies. And just seeing that it was capturing her attention the entire time, and then she held my hand and touched it and I could feel a little tear. And I was like, ’No way!’” Pattinson told the host.

Waterhouse doesn’t stand in rivalry with Pattinson’s famous exes.

After his tumultuous romance with Kristen Stewart, Pattinson gave all his heart to Waterhouse. As Pattinson’s second half, Waterhouse is now constantly followed by the paparazzi. They obviously get enough pics of the sweet couple kissing and want to find out all the details about their relationship and its secrets. However, after Stewart, Pattinson refuses to discuss if he is or isn’t dating someone.

Waterhouse, in her turn, doesn’t show off her boyfriend on her social networks, nor does she ever posts common shots together with him. She can easily be spotted vacationing with her friends, with Pattinson nowhere in sight.

Waterhouse has also had her fair share of relationship drama in the past, like with actor Bradley Cooper, with whom she often went out in public. But their 17-year age gap might have been the reason they parted after 2 years together. Leaving behind their past relationships, Pattinson and Waterhouse are more like a match made in heaven, and they look happier than ever.

Waterhouse once faced criticism over her attachment to Pattinson and bravely handled it.

Once, there was a pop culture reference to Waterhouse’s relationship with Pattinson in the new Gossip Girl reboot. There, a mean girl, Luna (Zión Moreno), references Waterhouse in one of the scenes.

“When are you going to get it? As far as the press is concerned, he’s R-Patz, and you’re Suki nobody,” Luna said about the relationship of a fictional couple.

Waterhouse’s reaction was strong and powerful. She wrote on her Twitter account, “Another day to be reminded that women can also be the patriarchy. Seeing critiques of the patriarchy and sexism, then I get name-checked as somebody’s ‘nobody’ girlfriend. Make it make sense!”
