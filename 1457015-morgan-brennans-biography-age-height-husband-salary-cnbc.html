She became a staff writer/ reporter for Forbes in January 2011. She reported, wrote, and edited articles for Forbes Magazine, ForbesLife Magazine, and Forbes.com. She also produced and anchored videos for Forbes.com and compiled special packages. She left Forbes in November 2013.
