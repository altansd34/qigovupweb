'The Voice' Country Star, BIAS is Set to Perform at Blake Shelton's Ole Red Bar

Posted by Tandra Barner on Tuesday, August 27, 2024

TALENTRECAP.COM Singing The Voice

Photo by: Trae Patton/NBC

Reading Time: 2 minutes

Fans of The Voice are in for an exciting musical experience as standout performer BIAS is scheduled to take the stage at Blake Shelton’s famous Ole Red bar. The rising country star will bring his powerful vocals and high-energy show to the venue’s “Team Gwen Doghouse Party” event.

BIAS Set to Make The Ole Red Debut

The announcement on Ole Red‘s Instagram page generated plenty of enthusiasm, revealing BIAS as one of the headliners for upcoming shows at The Doghouse. This performance on February 10, 2024, comes on the heels of his acclaimed run on The Voice, where he wowed audiences as a member of Team Gwen.

BIAS made his mark on the competition with his gritty country vocals, showstopping covers, and magnetic charisma. Now, fans will get to see him live in concert at the iconic Ole Red in Tishomingo, Oklahoma. For many, this will be the first chance to see BIAS since his national TV debut.

The Team Gwen Doghouse Party is shaping up to be an incredible full experience for BIAS fans. Scheduled for Saturday, February 10, 2024, the evening kicks off at 6:30 PM with dinner served at 7 PM. BIAS will then take the stage at 8 PM to deliver a high-energy full-band show.

The ticket price includes admission, dinner, and also the concert, making it an unbeatable value. Fans can look forward to an amazing meal and then followed by BIAS’s impressive vocals and stage presence. With his soulful tone and dynamic range, he’s sure to impress the Ole Red crowd.

The venue promises a memorable night out for ages 16 and up. Tickets are available via the link in Ole Red’s Instagram bio, ranging from $54.00 to $75.00. Space is limited, so fans should secure their spots soon for this can’t-miss evening.

BIAS is Going on Tour

Leading up to his Ole Red gig, BIAS shared additional tour dates on his own Instagram. Prior stops include shows in Chattanooga, Tennessee, Batesville, Arkansas, and also Lawrenceburg, Tennessee.

This string of performances marks an important moment as BIAS branches out following his national exposure on The Voice. He expressed gratitude for the chance to do what he loves, writing in his tour announcement post: “So incredibly grateful for the opportunities coming my way.”

The Ole Red show will cap off this initial tour run, with BIAS primed to deliver his best for Shelton‘s lively crowd. Fans in Oklahoma are thrilled that his journey includes a stop at the iconic bar and music venue.
