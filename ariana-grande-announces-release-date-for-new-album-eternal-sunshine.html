Ariana Grande has finally announced a release date for her upcoming seventh album. Fans were correct in guessing that the project will be called Eternal Sunshine. Grande recently released the album’s lead single, “Yes, And?”

Ariana Grande Announces Album Release Date

Earlier this month, eagle-eyed fans figured out that Grande’s new album title would be a reference to the movie Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind. As expected, Grande announced on Wednesday that the album is called Eternal Sunshine and will be released March 8.

When announcing the release of “Yes, And?” earlier this month, Grande shared what she said was one of a few different album covers. She included that image in her announcement of the album release date, along with two others.

In one shot, Grande can be seen wearing a white blouse and red gloves, with her hands touching her forehead. In another shot, Grande puts her gloved hands over her eyes while smiling. These are presumably the other two covers for the album.

Grande Released the Song ‘Yes, And?’ Last Week

Last week, Grande released the lead single for Eternal Sunshine, titled “Yes, And?” She also put out a music video for the upbeat track, in which she shows off a stylish dance routine for a group of critics, some of whom join in.

As she sings on the track’s chorus, “‘Yes, and?’ / Say that sh*t with your chest, and / Be your own f*ckin’ best friend / Say that sh*t with your chest / Keep moving like, ‘What’s next?’ / ‘Yes, and?’”

This is Grande’s first new album since 2020’s Positions. The singer has been busy with other commitments in recent years, including coaching on The Voice and starring in a movie adaptation of Wicked.
