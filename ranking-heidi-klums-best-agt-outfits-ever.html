Ranking Heidi Klum's Best 'AGT' Outfits Ever

Posted by Billy Koelling on Monday, August 12, 2024

TALENTRECAP.COM Talent America's Got Talent

Photo by Amy Sussman/Getty Images

Reading Time: 4 minutes

Heidi Klum is a fashion icon and supermodel known for her impeccable sense of style. Her role as a judge on the hit reality show, America’s Got Talent, has given us plenty of opportunities to see her stunning outfits on the screen. From glamorous gowns to daring and unique ensembles, Klum has certainly made her mark in the fashion world on and off the runway.

Whether she’s on the stage or sitting at the judges’ table, Klum’s fashion choices always leave a lasting impression. So, without further ado, let’s dive into the top ten Klum outfits on AGT.

Heidi Klum’s Best ‘AGT’ Outfits Ever, Ranked

10. Full Denim Maxi Dress

Klum looked stunning in her full denim outfit while shooting AGT: All-Stars in Pasadena, California. The sleeveless maxi dress made entirely out of medium-wash denim fabric featured wide shoulder straps, pockets at the chest and the back, and belt loops. She paired the outfit with gold stiletto heels and accessorized with large gold hoop earrings, a gold wraparound bracelet, and various gold rings.

9. White Satin Suit

Klum rocked a white satin suit during the AGT season 17 live shows. The ensemble consisted of a sharp double-breasted blazer jacket with wide lapels and a pointy hemline, paired with a vest and matching pleated high-waist trousers. She accessorized the look with long diamond chandelier earrings and a chunky sparkling ring, while her straight hair was styled with tapered bangs framing her face.

8. Black Rhinestone Gown

Klum wore a black rhinestone gown with sparkly diamante cross straps while filming the AGT season 17 finale. The dress showed off her toned legs, and she completed the all-black look with strappy high heels and a diamond bracelet. Klum wore her hair back and complemented the look with dark makeup around the eyes and a matte nude lipstick.

7. Floral Outfit

During season 16 of AGT, the supermodel stepped out in a Dolce and Gabbana matching set. She wore a halter top and high-waisted pants ensemble that mixed complementary prints, including floral, lace, cheetah, and baroque. Klum’s Dolce and Gabbana matching set showcased her impeccable taste and fearless style, and she pulled it off effortlessly.

6. See-Through Gown

Klum stunned in a daring see-through gown on AGT. The two-toned dress, made from green panels covered in grommets, showed off her toned figure and fashion-forward sensibility. Her bold fashion choice once again proves why she is a fashion icon.

5. Sequin Floral Dress

Klum looked amazing wearing her outfit on season 17 of AGT, which was a Dolce & Gabbana sequin-embellished floral-patterned mini dress that accentuated her figure perfectly. The dress featured intricate details and a vibrant floral pattern that was eye-catching and memorable. Klum’s fashion sense was once again on point, and she showcased her impeccable style with elegance and grace.

4. Green Sequined Suit

Klum stole the show at AGT season 17 kickoff event in Pasadena, Calif., with her glittery arrival on the red carpet. The judge looked radiant in a vivid green sequin suit, featuring an oversized blazer and also matching pants that exuded sophistication and glamour. Her look was completed with green futuristic shades and the Dolce & Gabbana’s Printed Elaphe Baroque DG sandals, which added an extra level of extravagance and made her outfit even more unforgettable.

3. Blue, Pink Patterned Mini Dress

Klum flaunted her toned legs in a pink and blue patterned Dolce and Gabbana mini dress that perfectly complemented her bronzed skin. The AGT judge radiated elegance and beauty as she walked the red carpet at the Sheraton Pasadena Hotel. Posing for the cameras with confidence and grace. Klum completed her outfit with a pair of strappy gold sandals that added a touch of glamour to her look. Not to mention her dramatic smokey eye makeup gave her an edgy and sultry vibe.

2. Black Suit Dress

Klum proved once again that she is a style icon on the AGT red carpet in Los Angeles, where she made a powerful statement in a black suit dress by David Koma. The dress featured a glittery bra cup that added a touch of sophistication and edginess to her look. While her equally glittery strappy heeled sandals complemented the dress perfectly.

1. Glitter Mini Dress

Klum turned heads at the finale of AGT in a glittery, animal-print mini dress by designer Julien Macdonald. She paired the statement dress with fishnet tights, shoulder-length sparkly earrings, and dramatic makeup featuring black smokey eyes and an extra thick cat-eye wing. The supermodel then showed off her signature sexy style with confidence and looked stunning from head to toe.
