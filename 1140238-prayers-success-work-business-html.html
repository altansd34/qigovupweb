Prayers for success in work and business

Posted by Billy Koelling on Tuesday, August 13, 2024

Do you need some prayer points for success in your business or job? Below are some powerful prayers for success that you can pray at home or at your place of work.

Committing our work or business into God’s hands is very important. We should not think we can succeed on our own. That is why the wise King Solomon said in Proverbs 3 verses 5 and 6:

“Trust in the Lord with all your heart; do not depend on your own understanding. Seek his will in all you do, and he will show you which path to take.” New Living Translation.

Below are some powerful prayers for success and prosperity in your business and in your work.

Prayers for success and prosperity in business

  • Dear Lord, I thank you for this opportunity to manage this business. I pray for your wisdom as I plan to work hard so that my business will remain secure and be prosperous. I pray that you grant me the strength and grace to make good practice the cornerstone of all that I do in this business. Help me to see open doors of opportunity for expansion and development of this business. I pray that my business will grow and flourish so that it can create great opportunities for everyone involved in Jesus Name. Amen.
  • Father, I commit my business into your hands. I pray for your guidance in all that I do as I build and shape my business. I cover my business and investments with the blood of Jesus. I pray that your Holy Spirit will teach and advise me daily concerning my business. I receive a sound mind that I may envision that which will make my business prosper and be a blessing to many. And may my business be full of fruitfulness, generosity, integrity, and provision. I give you everything that I am and everything that I have for your glory Lord. In Jesus Name. Amen.

Help us to be successful at all that we do. Bless this day the works of our hands. I confess that without you I am nothing and that in you I put my trust completely. And as your word say that we should commit to you whatever we do and that you will establish our plans, I pray that you will establish my business and make it strong forever in Jesus Name. Amen.   • Father, as I daily meditate on your word day and night and I also strive to obey all that you have instructed me in your word, help me, Lord, to prosper in my ways and to have good success in my work in Jesus Name. Read Joshua 1 verse 8.
  • Father, I pray that from today, let all my investments and labour will flourish and yield fruits in Jesus Name.

Prayer for success at work

  • Father, I thank you for my place of work. I commit the place into your hands. And I go to work every day, let me always experience favour in Jesus Name.
  • Almighty God, the one who holds every matter of life, help me always to give careful thoughts and be attentive to my work so that I may succeed in it. Help me always to give my best and be successful at every duty that I am given in Jesus Name.
  • Lord, help me to do everything that I do at my workplace with excellence in Jesus Name. Grant me wisdom and understanding in my work so that I will always do my work efficiently in Jesus Name.
  • Lord, I come against anything that will hinder my success and promotion at my work in Jesus Name.
