Sydney Sweeney is Already Back in Australia For 'Anyone But You' Reshoots With Glen Powell

Posted by Patria Henriques on Tuesday, August 6, 2024

TALENTRECAP.COM Entertainment Celebrity

Corbis via Getty Images

Reading Time: 2 minutes

Sydney Sweeney and Glen Powell are currently down under in Australia, busy with reshoots for their upcoming rom-com Anyone But You. The 26-year-old actress was spotted back on set in Sydney, Australia just hours after the conclusion of the SAG-AFTRA strike.

Sydney Sweeney, Glen Powell Reshooting Additional Scenes For Anyone But You

Anyone But You revolves around Bea, who is played by Sweeney, and Ben, played by Powell, who appear to be the perfect couple until a twist of fate cools their fiery attraction. The official synopsis teases, “So they do what any two mature adults would do: pretend to be a couple.”

The film, set to hit theaters on December 22, has briefly resumed production to capture a few additional “beauty shots.” Despite the tight schedule, Sony Pictures seems ready to go, probably having awaited the opportunity to complete these reshoots at a moment’s notice. Powell is also in Australia, a fact he shared on his Instagram Stories.

The reshoots took place at Bondi Beach, following the resolution of the Screen Actors Guild-American Federation of Television and Radio Artists (SAG-AFTRA) strike on November 9, 2023. Director Will Gluck expressed his excitement about the strike ending in time for the additional scenes.

Fan accounts and Gluck himself shared some behind-the-scenes photos from the reshoots on social media platforms. The additional shots aim to enhance the visual appeal of the romantic comedy.

Fans Shared Mixed Reactions to Anyone But You Trailer

The first trailer for Anyone But You has sparked a lively debate among fans, primarily revolving around the perceived clichéd storyline and the choice of music, “I Hate The Way That I Love You” by Labyrinth. One user voiced an opinion shared by many, expressing concern about the apparent predictability of the plot.

“Ok, let me guess. They hate each other at first, they have to do something together and then fall in love in the end. Isn’t this formula overused?” The viewer commented.

“A simple change of music could’ve improved this trailer.” Another viewer wrote.

“How can such beautiful people have the chemistry of two neighboring broomsticks?” A third viewer wrote.

Despite the mixed reactions, there is anticipation and curiosity about the film, as evidenced by various fans expressing excitement and eagerness to see Sweeney and Powell in action. The diverse range of comments sets the stage for a film that is likely to spark discussions.
