15 Celebrity Siblings That Have Big Age Gaps

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, August 13, 2024

Many celebrities, like some of us, have been lucky enough to have siblings with whom to share their life or part of it. Who could imagine, for example, that a Marvel Avenger would spend a Saturday afternoon helping his brother with his homework? Well, that’s the case of many celebs you’ve probably seen in movies or heard on the radio, especially those that have siblings with big age gaps. After all, regardless of whether they are from the same blood or not, siblings will always be a source of inspiration and a good example.

That’s why Bright Side decided to make a compilation of 15 celebrity siblings that have more than the usual age gap and here’s what we found.

1. Kim Kardashian (39 Y.O.) and Kylie Jenner (23 Y.O.)

Kim might be the most famous Kardashian of them all, and she’s also the oldest sibling from the group of sisters after Kourtney. On the other hand, Kylie, who’s 17 years younger than Kim, was nominated by Forbes magazine as one of the richest people in the world in 2019.

2. Demi Lovato (28 Y.O.) and Madison De La Garza (18 Y.O.)

When Demi Lovato was barely 9 years old, her mother gave birth to her half-sister, and the resemblance between the 2 sisters is striking. Young Madison is a great animal enthusiast, she has even adopted dogs, fish, hamsters, parakeets, and rabbits.

3. Ariana Grande (27 Y.O.) and Frankie Grande (37 Y.O.)

Frankie, Ariana Grande’s older brother, is about a decade older than the successful pop singer. He is also in the business of acting and singing, but of course, in a totally different environment. Frankie Grande is mostly known for the roles he plays in theaters on Broadway.

4. Rihanna (32 Y.O.) and Rajad Fenty (24 Y.O.)

The singer of the song “Umbrella” is about 8 years older than her younger brother Rajad. He always posts pictures with the star on his social media channels and they seem to have a great relationship. Notice that Rihanna is not only loved by her family, but basically by all of Barbados, where February 22 is known as Rihanna Day.

5. Nicole Richie (39 Y.O.) and Sofia Richie (22 Y.O.)

6. Nick Jonas (28 Y.O.) and Franklin Jonas (20 Y.O.)

Nick Jonas’ younger brother was born 8 years after the singer. The Jonas Brothers often post pictures with Frankie on their social media channels, where you can see the good relationship they all have.

7. Chloë Grace Moretz (23 Y.O.) and Trevor Duke Moretz (34 Y.O.)

The actress is more than 10 years younger than her older brother, Trevor Duke Moretz, and is also the only woman in the group of 5 siblings.

8. Jesse Eisenberg (36 Y.O.) and Hallie Eisenberg (28 Y.O.)

Jesse Eisenberg is 9 years older than his sister Hallie, who has also been involved in acting since she was a child, decided to go in a different direction. In recent years she changed the Hollywood sets for something just as spectacular: Broadway.

9. Justin Bieber (26 Y.O.) and Jazmyn Bieber (12 Y.O.)

The singer has 2 sisters from different mothers and he actually has quite a big age difference with both of them: he is 14 years older than Jazmyn “Jazzy” Bieber and 24 years older than Bay. Justin Bieber often posts pictures with both of them on his Instagram profile and they often have cute and loving captions.

10. Zoë Kravitz (31 Y.O.), Lola (13 Y.O.) and Nakoa-Wolf Momoa (11 Y.O.)

Zoë is almost 20 years older than her younger half-siblings, Lola and Nakoa-Wolf Momoa. The actress, who is the stepdaughter of renowned actor Jason Momoa, shows on Instagram the good relationship she has with her stepdad and her siblings.

11. Britney Spears (38 Y.O.) and Jamie Lynn (29 Y.O.)

The Spears sisters have an age gap of 10 years, with Britney being the oldest sister. The great resemblance they bear is uncanny. They’re almost identical in every sense, even in height. Britney is 5’4″, and Jamie is just an inch taller.

12. James Franco (42 Y.O.) and Dave Franco (35 Y.O.)

Dave Franco is 7 years younger than his extremely well-known brother, James. However, we wouldn’t find it strange to hear people say that they are twins, as the resemblance between these 2 is striking. James is an animal enthusiast, and he shows it on his social networks.

13. Alexander Skarsgård (44 Y.O.) and Bill Skarsgård (30 Y.O.)

All members of the Skarsgård family are actors, even if Alexander could be the exception since he used to be a sailor before he entered showbiz. Between siblings, they have a notable difference in age, since Bill was born when his older brother was 14.

14. Chris Hemsworth (37 Y.O.) and Liam Hemsworth (30 Y.O.)

The Hemsworth brothers have a 7-year-age gap, but if you dig a little bit deeper, you’ll learn that the oldest of the 3, Luke, is 39. Both Chris and Liam were called to the casting for the role of Thor, and after getting a second session, Chris won the role.

15. Miley Cyrus (27 Y.O.) and Noah Cyrus (20 Y.O.)

Despite the fact that Miley Cyrus is about 8 years older than her little sister, they seem to have a very close relationship. They’re often seen attending galas together. In addition, Noah has collaborated with Miley on several artistic projects.

Do you think a big age difference between siblings can make bonding more difficult for them? Do you have a big age gap with your siblings? Let us know in the comments!

Rihanna’s philanthropic endeavors are making waves as she continues to leverage her influence for global good.
